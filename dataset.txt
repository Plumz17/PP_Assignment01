The stars are gone. 
The planets have disappeared.
They were barely enough of us left to give "the end" a name. 
The Quiet Rapture.
Supplies dwindle. 
Infrastructure crumbles. 
Too few to rebuild. 
Too many to feed. 
Humanity decays.
But don't despair, my sons. 
I tell you now that there is more to these moons than meets the eye.
The Consolidation, They hide their technology. 
Their people, They won't tell you what they found, But I will.
One moon stands apart from the rest.
And in the darkness of that moon, An ocean of blood.
